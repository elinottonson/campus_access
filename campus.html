About Campus Access

 

 

For UMass Students who are dissatisfied with accessibility, and upkeep of accessibility on campus, Campus Access is an interactive accessibility map that provides a unique experience by allowing you to report issues in real time, view floorplans with key accessibility features and track the flow of pedestrian traffic on campus. Unlike other competitors, we have assembled a real time, issue reporting system & in-building navigation to assure students with the most up to date campus hazard information, indoors and outdoors.

Features

Live Issue Reporting

Accessibilty on campus is only accessible if it's maintained. You can report accessibility issues on campus, informing other students as well as campus maintaince to fix it ASAP! After reporting an issue, if you are in need of help, the application will use your profile information to contact you and find the best way to help.  

AI Powered Campus Traffic Predictions

Know when to leave for class at the time you have to leave while avoiding crowds and following the most accessible path. The AI utilizes user location data to predict the most optimal route by factoring in university scheduling, weather, and more. 

Complete Voice Navigation

Our application is completly accessible through voice navigation to make sure it can be used with those with a wide variety of accessibility needs. 

In-Building Navigation

Not only navigate the best campus paths, but also inside buildings. Floor layouts, accessible doors, elevators, bathrooms - you can navigate them all.
